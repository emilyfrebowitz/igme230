Tiny Steam Engine

Role

3D Modeling

When

Fall 2017

Synopsis

The tiny steam engine was rendered in Cinema 4D. Design, model, rig, lighting and shot set-up all by me. Camera movement was an important dramatic element to this piece. It was especially inspired by organc shapes and a blend of earth and machinery, which led to color pallette choice. Forms in the engine are reminscent of ones found in nature.
