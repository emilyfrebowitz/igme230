NASA: Journey to Mars

Role

3D Modeling

When

Fall 2017

Synopsis

A school project, the goal of this project was a film about an event. I chose the upcoming NASA mission to mars with an interest in creating a space themed animation. I first created the NASA logo in 3D, using Cinema 4D, and then created a scene of reaching towards Mars. With some dramatic camera movements, spins, and camera angles, I attempted to create a dramatic and exciting piece about the upcoming event.
